How to play

Game Setup

Battleship is a game where you will take turns with an AI in order to attack one another. You both have 5 ships:

  • Battleship
  • Cruiser
  • Aircraft Carrier
  • Destroyer
  • Submarine

To begin, place all of your ships onto the board by ______________
Now the game can begin!

Playing the game

Now that the ships have been placed you may begin firing upon your opponent's board!
To do this, simply click on their board at the location you wish to fire.
If you hit an opponent's ship, an X will appear on the square that you fired at.
If you miss, an O will appear instead.

If you destroy all of your opponent's ships before they destroy yours, YOU WIN!!!
